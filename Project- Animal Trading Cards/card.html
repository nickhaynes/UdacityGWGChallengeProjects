Manatees

Manatees are believed to account for at least some of the earliest 'sightings' of mermaids.

  • Scientific Name: Trichechus
  • Average Length: 8.4 ft.
  • Average Lifespan: 40-60 years
  • Habitat: Coastal waters

Manatees are large aquatic mammels sometimes known as sea cows. They can measure up to 13.1 feet long and weigh as much as 1,300 pounds, have paddle-like flippers, and are mostly herbivorous.
